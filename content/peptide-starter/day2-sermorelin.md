> TL;DR: Start with 200 mcg Sermorelin 30–60 minutes before bed, away from food, five nights on/two nights off. Track IGF‑1 every 6 weeks, pair with sleep hygiene, and swap to nasal peptides if needles become the bottleneck.

## 1. Pull from the field notes

The February 6 WhatsApp stack laid out a classic beginner pathway: **Sermorelin 100–300 mcg, dosed nightly away from food**.* That cadence mirrors what most longevity clinics recommend—aligning with your natural slow-wave GH pulse and avoiding food-induced insulin spikes that blunt absorption.[1][2]

*WhatsApp Longevity Circle, Feb 6 2026.

## 2. Dosing ladder (first 16 weeks)

| Phase | Dose | Frequency | Rationale |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Weeks 1–4** | 200 mcg | Nightly, 30–60 min before bed | Conservative start while you dial in injections + watch glucose.[1][3]
| **Weeks 5–8** | 250 mcg (if IGF‑1 still low/mid) | Nightly, same window | Adjust only if IGF‑1 stays below age-adjusted median.[1]
| **Weeks 9–16** | 250–300 mcg | 5 nights on / 2 off | Introduce rest days to prevent pituitary fatigue; align with travel or social nights.[2][4]

**Needle SOP:**
1. Reconstitute 5 mg vial with 2.5 mL bacteriostatic water (2 mg/mL).
2. Draw 0.1–0.15 mL per dose; rotate abdomen/thigh sites.
3. Store vial at 2–8 °C, avoid shaking.

## 3. Pair with lifestyle guardrails

- **4 PM hydration cutoff** (from Jorn’s note) to reduce night-time urination and improve deep sleep.*
- **10 g glycine** + **magnesium** 60 minutes before bed to shorten sleep latency (also in the WhatsApp action list).
- **Soundcore Sleep A30** or any low-profile ANC buds for travel nights.*

These habits amplify the sleep-extension you’re chasing with GH peptides.

## 4. Monitoring stack

| Marker | Target | When |
| --- | --- | --- |
| IGF‑1 | Middle of age-adjusted range (not supra-physiologic) | Baseline, week 6, week 12.[1]
| Fasting glucose + HbA1c | No upward drift (>5% from baseline) | Baseline, week 8, week 16.[1]
| AST/ALT | Stay <2x baseline | Baseline + quarterly.
| Sleep metrics (WHOOP/Oura) | +15–20 min deep sleep vs. baseline | Weekly trendline.
| Subjective log | Rate recovery, mood, appetite | Daily.

Wave the yellow flag if IGF‑1 > 350 ng/mL, fasting glucose creeps above 95 mg/dL, or edema/headaches show up after dose bumps.

## 5. “No-needle” alternates if you stall

Not everyone wants to pin nightly. Offer alternatives early:

- **Intranasal Semax/Selank** for cognitive + stress benefits (still off-label; emphasize compliance and sourcing).[5]
- **Topical GHK-Cu + SNAP-8**: addresses hair/skin goals from the WhatsApp note without injections, with human data for hair density gains over 12 weeks.[6]
- **Lifestyle mimic**: Evening sauna + cold plunge to naturally encourage GH pulses.

Use these as on-ramps before you fully commit to injectables or as travel substitutes.

## 7. Safety disclaimer

Sermorelin is FDA-approved only for pediatric GH deficiency; all adult use is off-label. Work with a clinician, do not exceed recommended doses, and avoid stacking with GH analogs unless supervised.
