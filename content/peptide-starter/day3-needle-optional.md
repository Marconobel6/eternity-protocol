## 2. Semax: cognitive & neuroprotective focus

- **Mechanism:** ACTH(4-7) analog that upregulates BDNF and NGF; studied in Russia for post-stroke recovery and cognitive support.[1][2]
- **Delivery:** Intranasal spray avoids the blood-brain barrier, providing fast onset.[2]
- **Beginner protocol:** Reconstitute lyophilized vial (1–2 mg) with bacteriostatic water; use a sterile nasal sprayer. Start with **1 spray/nostril twice daily** (approx. 250–500 mcg total) for 10–14 days, then take 7 days off.[2]
- **Use cases:** Learning blocks, jet lag, heavy strategy weeks.

## 3. Selank: anxiolytic + stress modulation

- **Mechanism:** Tuftsin analog that modulates serotonin and GABA signaling, lowering cortisol without sedation.[1][2]
- **Beginner protocol:** 2 sprays/nostril, three times daily (spread across morning/afternoon/evening). Cycle 14 days on, 7 days off.[2]
- **Pairing:** Stack with Semax (morning cognitive boost, evening anti-anxiety) but introduce them one at a time.

## 4. Safety + sourcing guardrails

- Neither peptide is FDA-approved; they’re sold as research chemicals only. Label articles with a compliance banner.
- Require proof of supplier COA + endotoxin testing.
- Emphasize intranasal hygiene: saline rinse, replace spray heads every cycle, refrigerate vials.
- Track mood, HRV, and sleep latency to catch overuse.

## 5. GHK-Cu + SNAP-8 (topical aesthetics stack)

The WhatsApp note suggests **GHK-Cu (1 mg) + 25 mg oral zinc** for hair and **SNAP-8** for skin.* External research backs it:

- **GHK-Cu**: Clinical data shows 2% topical solutions increased hair count, thickness, and density after 12 weeks; boosts collagen and angiogenesis while lowering inflammation.[3][4]
- **SNAP-8**: Octapeptide that reduces expression wrinkles by modulating SNARE complex activity; strong cosmetic-instrument data for wrinkle depth reduction (use as serum around eyes/forehead).

**Usage blueprint:**
1. Microneedle scalp (0.5 mm) twice weekly, apply GHK-Cu serum post-session.
2. Daily leave-in application along thinning areas; expect visible changes at 3–6 months.
3. SNAP-8 morning/night on clean skin; pair with sunscreen.
